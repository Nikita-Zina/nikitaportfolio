About



Roselynn Interiors operates as a distinguished interior design studio based in Hertfordshire, specialising in the creation of innovative and highly customised interiors for private residences and boutique commercial projects. Guided by founder and creative director Rosie Wilson, our studio's overarching aesthetic revolves around seamlessly blending traditional and contemporary elements. We employ a rich interplay of texture and colour to introduce unexpected and delightful details. Rosie Wilson, a 2021 graduate with Distinction from the renowned KLC School of Design in Chelsea, London, earned a special commendation for her outstanding performance as one of the top students in her class. Her approach involves integrating timeless pieces with a passion for discovering unique and unconventional elements.

Reflecting on my childhood, a profound fascination with the world of interiors has been a constant. My professional journey has encompassed diverse experiences, ranging from employment at The Guardian and Observer newspaper to teaching Pilates. This varied background has equipped me with a wealth of skills and knowledge. As an interior designer, I skillfully merge creativity and practicality, underpinned by strong organisational acumen. My role revolves around understanding the essence of how individuals live, work, rest and play within their homes. I firmly advocate for homes to serve as authentic representations of our characters, steering clear of the impersonal showroom aesthetic.
